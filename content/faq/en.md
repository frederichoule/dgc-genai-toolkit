## Understanding Generative AI impact

### What is the environmental impact of generative AI (GenAI)?

Generative AI requires significant computing power to process prompts and generate outputs. This computing relies on data centers that consume electricity and water — including end-users devices — resulting in greenhouse gas emissions. The impact varies widely depending on the type of task, model, and infrastructure used.

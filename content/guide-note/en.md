---
title: Note about AI use cases
---

This toolkit does not prescribe specific use cases for generative AI tools. These technologies can be applied to a wide range of creative and technical contexts, from early development to post-production, with approaches that vary significantly across projects.

The purpose of this toolkit is to create a better understanding of the environmental impact of generative AI use, regardless of how it is integrated into the creative process. Our focus is on supporting informed decision-making, not defining how such tools should be used.
